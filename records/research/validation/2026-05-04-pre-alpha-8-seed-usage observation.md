THE MUSCLE PT — 60-MINUTE TRAINING PROTOCOL TEMPLATE v1
Grounding: PMID 41843416 | https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41843416/ ACSM 2026 Position Stand: ≥2x/week per major muscle group, ≥80% 1RM for strength, ≥10 sets/muscle/week for hypertrophy, near-failure (~2–3 RIR) sufficient, full ROM, key lifts early.

Working set structure (locked): 1 max-effort set + 1 back-off set per major muscle group, preceded by 2 submax warm-up sets on the primary lift of each block.

SESSION ARCHITECTURE — 60 MINUTES
Block	Duration	Purpose
1. Pre-activation	10 min	Postural reset, breathing, dynamic mobility
2. Resistance training	30 min	Major muscle group work (max-effort + back-off)
3. Specificity	10 min	Isometrics / plyo / power / balance / rehab / sport
4. Cardio	10 min HIIT or 20 min moderate (replaces specificity on cardio-priority days)	Conditioning
Cardio modality choice:

10-min HIIT → fits inside the 60-min envelope, stacks after RT.
20-min moderate → requires extending session to 70 min OR replacing the specificity block.
BLOCK 1 — PRE-ACTIVATION (10 MIN)
Targets the common postural pattern: forward head, rounded shoulders, thoracic kyphosis, lumbar lordosis, anterior pelvic tilt.

Sequence (locked order, 90 sec each):

90/90 breathing — supine, feet on wall, knees over hips. 5 breaths. Reset rib position, kill lumbar extension.
Thoracic extension over foam roller — segmental, T4–T8. 8 reps. Reverses kyphosis.
Wall slides — back flat, arms in goalpost. 10 reps. Scap upward rotation, anti-rounding.
Hip flexor stretch with posterior pelvic tilt — half-kneeling. 30 sec/side. Counters APT.
Chin tuck + thoracic extension — seated or standing. 10 reps. Reverses FHP.
World's greatest stretch. 3/side. Full chain.
Glute bridge with exhale-hold. 8 reps. Reinforces PPT + diaphragm position.
Universal cues (apply across all 7 drills):

Points of contact: know what's touching the ground. Feel it before you move.
Stack: ribs over pelvis, ears over shoulders. Reset between every rep.
Breath: inhale 360° through nose to expand ribs, exhale through mouth to brace.
Tempo: 2 sec eccentric / 1 sec pause / 1 sec concentric on mobility work.
BLOCK 2 — RESISTANCE TRAINING (30 MIN)
Per major muscle group, per session:

2 warm-up sets (50%, 70% of working load)
1 max-effort set @ ≥80% 1RM, near-failure (2 RIR), full ROM
1 back-off set @ 70–75% of max-effort load, same rep target
Set timing budget: ~5 min per movement (warm-ups + working sets + rest). 6 movements max in 30 min.

Major muscle groups to cover ≥2x/week across the week: Chest · Back · Shoulders · Quads · Hamstrings/Glutes · Core

Universal RT cues:

Points of contact: plant before you press. Feet, hips, scaps, hands — all anchored.
Stack: ribs down, neutral spine, ears stacked over shoulders over hips.
Breath: inhale to brace at the top, exhale through the sticking point.
Tempo: 3-1-1-0 default (3 sec eccentric, 1 sec pause, 1 sec concentric, 0 sec top). Adjust per phase.
BLOCK 3 — SPECIFICITY (10 MIN)
Pick one modality per session based on client priority:

Modality	Default prescription
Isometrics	3 × 30 sec holds at end-range or sticking point
Plyometrics	4 × 5 reps, full recovery between sets
Power	3 × 3–5 reps @ 30–70% 1RM, max concentric intent
Balance	3 × 30 sec/side single-leg or unstable surface
Rehab	Client-specific corrective, 2–3 sets × 10–15 reps
Sport-specific	Pattern-matched to sport demand, 3–4 sets
Rotates by day across the week. On 2-day splits, prioritize the client's lowest-quality modality.

STANDARDIZED CUES — APPLY ACROSS ALL MODALITIES
1. Points of contact — Identify what's touching the ground or surface. Press into it before you move. Default for any standing movement: tripod foot (big toe, little toe, heel).

2. Maintain a relative stack — Ribs over pelvis. Ears over shoulders. Stack resets at the top of every rep. If the stack breaks, the rep ended one rep ago.

3. Breath control —

Inhale 360° through nose at the top.
Brace (think "press the belt outward").
Exhale through pursed lips on the concentric or sticking point.
4. Tempo —

Default: 3-1-1-0
Technique phase: 4-1-2-0
Intensity phase: 2-0-X-0
Read as: eccentric – pause – concentric – top
These four cue families apply to every block — pre-activation, RT, specificity, even cardio (especially HIIT intervals).

DEVIATION DISCLOSURE
This template uses 1 max-effort set + 1 back-off set instead of the ACSM-prescribed 2–3 working sets at ≥80% 1RM for strength. The 2x/week frequency and back-off set bring weekly volume toward the ≥10 sets/muscle/week hypertrophy threshold while preserving the 60-min envelope and the 1-max-effort-set intent.
