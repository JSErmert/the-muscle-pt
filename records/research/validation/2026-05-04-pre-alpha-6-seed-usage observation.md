THE MUSCLE PT — 60-MINUTE TRAINING PROTOCOL TEMPLATE v1
Grounding: PMID 41843416 | https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41843416/
ACSM 2026: ≥2x/week per major muscle group, ≥80% 1RM for strength (2–3 sets), ≥10 sets/muscle/week for hypertrophy, near-failure (~2–3 RIR) sufficient, full ROM, key lifts early.

RT PRESCRIPTION (per major muscle group, per session)

Warm-up: 2 submaximal sets, ascending load (~50% → ~70% 1RM)
Working set 1 (max effort): 1 set @ ≥80% 1RM, full ROM, ≤5 reps
Working set 2 (back-off): 1 set @ ~65–75% 1RM, 6–10 reps
Frequency: each major muscle group hit ≥2x/week
Eccentric overload: available as hypertrophy lever (3–4 sec eccentric on back-off set)

Volume disclosure: ACSM 2026 prescribes 2–3 working sets at ≥80% 1RM for strength and ≥10 sets/week for hypertrophy. This template's 1 max-effort + 1 back-off (2 working sets total) sits at the floor of the strength range and is sub-threshold for hypertrophy unless frequency lifts weekly volume to ≥10 sets/muscle. 2x/week × 2 working sets = 4 sets/week per muscle. To reach hypertrophy threshold, compensate with: (a) added accessory volume, (b) higher frequency for priority muscles, or (c) explicit acceptance that this template prioritizes strength over hypertrophy.

CUE STANDARDIZATION (apply across all modalities)
Five standardized cues. Use these exact phrases.
1. Points of contact
"Three points down: feet, ribs, head" (supine) / "Tripod foot: big toe, pinky toe, heel" (standing) / "Hands flat, knuckles down" (push)
2. Relative stack
"Stack ribs over hips, hips over heels"
3. Breath control
"Inhale to brace, exhale through the hard part" (concentric) / "Belly breath, not chest" (any setup)
4. Tempo
"3 down, 1 hold, 1 up" (default) / "Slow eccentric, controlled concentric" (back-off set)
5. Bracing
"360 brace — front, sides, back tight" before load transfer
