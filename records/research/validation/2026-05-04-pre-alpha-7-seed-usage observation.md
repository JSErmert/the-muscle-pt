THE MUSCLE PT — 60-MINUTE TRAINING PROTOCOL TEMPLATE v1
Time Architecture (60 min, all splits)
BlockDurationPurposePre-activation10 minPostural correction + breathing + dynamic mobilityResistance training~30 minMajor muscle groups (variable by split)Cardio10 min HIIT or 20 min moderate (choose 1)ConditioningSpecificity10 minIsometrics / plyo / power / balance / rehab / sport
If cardio = 20 min moderate, RT block compresses to ~20 min. 4-day and 5-day splits absorb this better than 2-day.

RT Prescription (per major muscle group, per session)

Warm-up: 2 submaximal sets, ascending load (~50% → ~70% 1RM)
Working set 1 (max effort): 1 set @ ≥80% 1RM, full ROM, 3–5 reps, ~1–2 RIR
Working set 2 (back-off): 1 set @ ~65–75% 1RM, 6–10 reps, ~2–3 RIR
Frequency: each major muscle group ≥2x/week
Eccentric overload: 3–4 sec eccentric on back-off set as hypertrophy lever

Volume disclosure: ACSM 2026 prescribes 2–3 working sets at ≥80% 1RM for strength and ≥10 sets/week per muscle for hypertrophy. This template's 2 working sets × 2x/week = 4 sets/week per muscle — at strength floor, sub-threshold for hypertrophy. To reach hypertrophy threshold for priority muscles: (a) add accessory volume in the specificity block, (b) bump frequency to 3x/week on 4-day or 5-day splits, or (c) accept this template prioritizes strength over hypertrophy.

Universal Cue Stack (applies across all modalities)
Points of contact (cue at every working set setup):

Feet: tripod — big toe, pinky toe, heel
Hips/pelvis: neutral, ribcage stacked over pelvis
Shoulders: packed — back and down, not shrugged
Head: neutral — chin slightly tucked, ears over shoulders

Relative stack: ribcage over pelvis over feet. If stack breaks under load, load is too high.
Breath control:

Brace before lift: nasal inhale, 360° belly expansion, exhale 20% to lock
Concentric: exhale through pursed lips
Eccentric: hold or slow nasal inhale
Reset between reps: full nasal breath cycle

Tempo standard: 3-1-1-0 (3 sec eccentric, 1 sec bottom, 1 sec concentric, 0 sec top) on max-effort working set. Back-off set: 4-0-1-0 with eccentric overload focus.

Cardio Block
Choose one per session, not both.
HIIT (10 min): 30 sec max effort / 90 sec recovery × 5 rounds. Modalities: assault bike, rower, sled push, hill sprint. Cue: "All-out work. Full recovery. Don't hold back the work to save the recovery."
Moderate (20 min): Zone 2 — nasal-only breathing, conversational pace, HR ~60–70% max. Modalities: incline walk, easy bike, swim. Cue: "If you can't hold a full sentence on nasal breath, slow down."

Specificity Block (10 min — modality rotates by client need)
Pick one focus per session:

Isometrics: 5 × 30 sec holds at end-range or sticking-point joint angle
Plyometrics: 4 × 5 max-intent jumps (box, broad, depth) — full recovery between sets
Power: 3 × 3 explosive lifts at 30–70% 1RM (med ball throw, jump squat, kettlebell swing)
Balance: 4 × 30 sec single-leg, eyes-closed or unstable surface progressions
Rehab: targeted weak-link work (rotator cuff, glute med, deep neck flexor, etc.)
Sport-specific: movement pattern from the client's sport, low load, high intent

Periodization (12-week macrocycle)
Weeks 1–4 — Technique mastery

Load held at moderate (~70% 1RM ceiling on max-effort set)
Tempo emphasis: 4-1-2-0 (slow eccentric, controlled concentric)
Add reps only if every rep meets cue stack
Failure point: cue compliance, not load

Weeks 5–8 — Progressive overload

Load progresses to ≥80% 1RM on max-effort set
Add 2.5–5 lb when previous session's top set hit prescribed reps with ≤1 RIR
Back-off set: add reps before adding load
Failure point: rep target with clean technique

Weeks 9–12 — Intensity push

Max-effort set: 85–95% 1RM, 1–3 reps, 0–1 RIR
Back-off set: drop to 70% 1RM, push to 2 RIR
Specificity block emphasizes power/plyo to express the strength built
Failure point: technical breakdown — hold load, don't push past it

Reassess at week 12: deload week, retest 1RM or e1RM, restart cycle with new baseline.

Modification Triggers (when to deviate from template)

Technique degrades on warm-up sets → hold load, do not advance
Pain (not soreness) on a movement → swap to regression, flag for assessment
Two consecutive sessions miss rep target → deload that lift 10%, rebuild
Postural pattern not addressed by default pre-activation → swap in client-specific corrective in min 2:00–4:00 slot
Sport in-season → cut RT volume 30%, preserve max-effort set, drop back-off set
