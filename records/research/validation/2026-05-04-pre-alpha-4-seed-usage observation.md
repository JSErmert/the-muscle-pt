STANDARDIZED CUE STACK (Apply across all modalities)
Points of contact (universal stack):

Feet — tripod (big toe, pinky toe, heel) for standing; full foot pressed for seated/supine
Hips — neutral pelvis, ribs stacked over pelvis (no flare, no tuck)
Ribs — exhale-locked position, lats engaged
Shoulders — packed (depressed + slight retraction), not shrugged
Head — chin tucked, ears over shoulders

Relative stack cue (single phrase for all lifts):
"Ribs over hips, hips over heels."
Breath control:

Exhale on exertion (concentric)
Inhale on lowering (eccentric)
For max-effort sets >80% 1RM: brace + Valsalva on rep, exhale at top
360° brace cue: "Pressure into the belt, not just the front."

Tempo standard:

Strength sets (≥80% 1RM): 3-1-X-1 (3-sec eccentric, 1-sec pause, explosive concentric, 1-sec top)
Hypertrophy sets: 2-0-1-0 (controlled, no pause, smooth turnaround)
Power sets: X-0-X-0 (max intent both directions, no pause)

Range of motion standard: Full ROM unless pathology dictates otherwise — per ACSM 2026.
