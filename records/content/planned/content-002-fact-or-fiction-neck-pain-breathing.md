## Reel Script

`[TEXT ON SCREEN: "Fact or Fiction?"]`

### HOOK
*"Your neck pain has nothing to do with your breathing."*

`[TEXT ON SCREEN: "FICTION"]`

### VERDICT
*"Fiction."*

### MECHANISM
*"The muscles that move your ribs to breathe — your diaphragm, your intercostals, your scalenes — connect directly to your cervical spine and your upper thoracic region. Restrict the breathing, and you load the neck. Disable the neck, and the breathing weakens with it."*

`[TEXT ON SCREEN: "Diaphragm. Intercostals. Scalenes."]`

*"Your diaphragm doesn't just move air. It's a structural floor for the trunk. When the breathing pattern shifts to short and shallow, the load redistributes upward to the neck, and the small accessory muscles compensate. They were not built for it."*

### RESEARCH
*"In chronic non-specific neck pain, chest expansion correlates with respiratory capacity at r = 0.42. And the more disabled the neck feels, the weaker the respiratory pressures become — r = -0.58 for maximum expiratory pressure, r = -0.46 for maximum inspiratory pressure. The neck and the breathing are the same system."*

`[TEXT ON SCREEN: "r = -0.58 | r = -0.46"]`

### TIE IN
*"If your neck pain has been chronic, screen your breathing. Track chest expansion. Restore thoracic mobility. The breathing pattern is part of the neck's recovery, not a side concern."*

### SIGN OFF
*"Fiction. Your breathing belongs in your neck plan."*

`[TEXT ON SCREEN: "Fiction confirmed."]`

## How This Piece Exercises the Citation Contract

This is the first Bucket 3 piece — and Bucket 3 is the strictest bucket for citation. The verdict requires direct figure-backed support. The original trigger-phrase candidate ("Stretching your neck fixes neck pain") could not pass this bar. The pivot to "Your neck pain has nothing to do with your breathing" works because Wirth's correlations directly support the verdict:

| Verdict component | Wirth figure |
|---|---|
| Breathing and neck pain are connected (not separate) | Chest expansion ↔ MVV: r = 0.42 |
| Connection scales with disability severity | Pemax ↔ NDI: r = -0.58 |
| Inspiratory side also tracks | Pimax ↔ NDI: r = -0.46 |

Three independent figures support the same verdict from three different physiological measures. That is the kind of multi-figure backing Bucket 3's contract is designed to surface.
