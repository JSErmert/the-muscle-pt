# THE MUSCLE PT — EXECUTION PLAYBOOK v2

## Purpose

This document defines how work gets done daily and weekly.

It translates the system into:
- one clear action at a time
- repeatable workflows
- minimal decision-making during execution

The goal:
- the founder never figures out from scratch what to do next
- signal is captured without slowing execution
- the day compounds into structured intelligence

This is the **user-facing execution layer** of the system.

---

## Core Principle

Do the work.
Use the system to guide, not slow.
Capture real signal as it happens.
Let the day preserve itself in structure, not memory.

---

## 1. MORNING — PRIORITY ACTIVATION

Start the day with clarity, not thinking.

The system surfaces only:
- the highest-priority case or task
- one relevant insight (if applicable)
- one active friction signal (if applicable)
- one clear next action

Output format (strict):
- what matters
- why it matters (1 sentence)
- what to do next (1 action)

Success condition: the founder does NOT ask "what should I do today?"

---

## 2. DURING WORK — SIGNAL CAPTURE

Capture meaningful events without slowing execution.

Trigger events — log when something is:
- operationally meaningful
- repeatedly observed
- emotionally charged (friction, breakthrough, confusion)
- relevant to clients, content, or business direction

Each event becomes (internal classification):
- Case
- Insight
- Friction
- Ignore (no log)

Behavior:
- encourage capture when signal is high
- avoid logging noise

Success condition: no important signal lost; no unnecessary logging burden created.

---

## 3. REAL-TIME EXECUTION — ACTION GUIDANCE

When prompted, the system returns:
- one clear next step
- grounded in case, insight, or priority

Constraints:
- no multiple options unless explicitly requested
- no abstract explanation
- no system terminology

Success condition: every response leads to immediate action OR justified non-action.

---

## 4. END OF DAY — SIGNAL COMPRESSION (≤ 5 minutes)

Convert the day into retained intelligence.

Required outputs:
1. one key insight
2. one repeated pattern (if present)
3. one notable case (if applicable)

Storage: `records/system-history/extracted/`
Format: per `systems/intelligence/system-history-extraction-format-v1.md`

Constraints:
- no long journaling
- no over-analysis
- only highest-value signal

The day is preserved in structure, not memory.

---

## 5. CONTENT EXTRACTION (AS NEEDED)

Content must always come from:

```text
Case → Insight → Content
```

Never allowed:
- inventing content without source
- disconnected ideas

Each content piece must include:
- source_case_id
- source_insight_id

Workflow:
1. Select insight from `records/system-history/extracted/` or directly from a case
2. Identify content bucket
3. Choose hook format
4. Write script
5. Write caption
6. Film and edit
7. Post → move to `records/content/published/`
8. After 7 days → log to `records/content/performance/`

Posting rules:
- prioritize clarity over perfection
- prioritize real insight over invention
- never post low-quality content to maintain frequency

---

## 6. WEEKLY REVIEW — DIRECTION CONTROL (≤ 15 minutes)

Replace reactive thinking with evidence-based direction.

Required outputs:
- what repeated ≥ 2x
- what moved the business forward
- what consumed energy without leverage
- what content performed (after 7-day lag where applicable)
- what friction is now real (≥ 2 occurrences)
- score the week 1–5 across 4 health domains: execution / usage / decision / structural

System responsibilities:
- identify patterns
- identify friction
- classify system health
- suggest direction changes ONLY if justified

Output location: `records/logs/reviews/weekly/YYYY-WW.md`

Strategy becomes evidence-led, stable, iterative — NOT emotional, reactive, random.

---

## 7. PRIORITY RULE — WHEN UNSURE

If unsure what to do:
- follow `governance/prioritization-queue-engine-v1.md`
- work on the highest-priority item

---

## 8. FAILURE RULE — WHEN UNCLEAR

If something feels unclear:
- do not force clarity
- mark as inconclusive
- gather more data

---

## FOUNDER-FACING OUTPUT RULES

All outputs must follow:

### 1. No system language
Avoid: *case, classification, framework, engine, HL-NN, DP-NN, ET-NN, M1, M2*.

### 2. One clear action
Default = single next step.

### 3. Minimal explanation
Only include reasoning if necessary for action.

### 4. No overload
Never present:
- multiple competing paths
- excessive options
- abstract theory

---

## WHAT THE SYSTEM REPLACES

### Before
Wake up → think from scratch → feel friction → improvise → produce unevenly → lose signal

### After
Wake up → see priority → act → capture → compound

---

## WHAT SUCCESS FEELS LIKE (Founder Perspective)

- "I know exactly what to do next"
- "I don't have to think as much"
- "Everything feels more intentional"
- "I'm not starting over every day"
- "My work is building on itself"

---

## SYSTEM BOUNDARIES

The system must NOT:
- require the founder to manage it
- expose internal complexity
- introduce new tools mid-M1
- expand scope without recorded failure evidence (per HL-10)

---

## FAILURE INDICATORS

Log to `records/logs/incidents/` if:
- founder asks repeatedly what to do
- outputs feel abstract or unclear
- actions are not executable
- content lacks traceable source
- daily capture is skipped

Recurring failure (≥ 2x of the same type) → escalation candidate per `governance/escalation-triggers-v1.md`.

---

## DEFINITION OF SUCCESS

The system is working when:
- the founder moves faster with less thinking
- actions are clearer day-to-day
- signal is consistently captured
- insights begin repeating naturally
- weekly reviews produce real direction

This system turns daily experience into structured direction so the founder can move forward without repeatedly figuring things out.
